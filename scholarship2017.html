IGSA SCHOLARSHIP

The Indian Graduate Students Association (IGSA) Scholarship will be awarded to one student annually through the Department of Scholarship and Financial Aid.

The scholarship comes with an award of $1000 along with a non-resident tuition fee waiver.

Selection criteria:

Recipients must be continuing full-time students in good academic standing at Texas A&M University, College Station and must have demonstrated significant contribution to the Indian Community on campus through leadership, voluntary work and participation.

Donating to the IGSA Scholarship:
  • Online Payment :
    • Log on to givenow.tamu.edu.
    • Under Designations, you will select “contribute to a fund not listed online” and then enter either the :
      • Account number: 04-35950 or
      • Account Name : IGSA Scholarship
  • Pay via check:
    Donations are also accepted through check. The check can be addressed to Texas A&M foundation. And you can inform the association about the check and one of our associates would drop by your location to pick up the check.
